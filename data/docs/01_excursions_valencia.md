# Excursions in Valencia — Asturio Club

Asturio Club organizes guided excursions in Valencia for adults, families, and children. 
All excursions are led by experienced guides and focus on culture, history, gastronomy, and local traditions.

## Popular Excursions

### Old Town of Valencia
A walking tour through the historical center of Valencia, including:
- Plaza de la Virgen
- Valencia Cathedral
- La Lonja de la Seda (UNESCO World Heritage site)
- Central Market

Duration: 2–2.5 hours  
Format: walking tour  
Languages: Russian, English

### City of Arts and Sciences
A modern architecture tour covering:
- Hemisfèric
- Science Museum
- Oceanogràfic (external visit)

This tour focuses on modern Valencia, science, and architecture.

Duration: 2 hours  
Suitable for families and teenagers.

### Citrus Farm Excursion
An out-of-town excursion to a local citrus farm near Valencia.
Participants learn about:
- orange and mandarin cultivation
- seasonal harvest
- local agricultural traditions

Includes tasting of seasonal fruits.

Duration: 3–4 hours  
Best season: October – April

## Group Size
Typical group size is 5–15 people. Private tours are available upon request.

## What Is Included
- Professional guide
- Excursion program
- Assistance with transportation planning

Transportation and entrance tickets may not be included unless specified.
