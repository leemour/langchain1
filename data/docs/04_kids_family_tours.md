# Family and Kids Tours — Asturio Club

Asturio Club offers excursions and tours designed specifically for families with children.

## Kids-Friendly Tours

### Interactive City Quests
City-based quests where children:
- solve riddles
- explore landmarks
- learn history through games

Suitable for children aged 6–12.

### Farm Visits
Visits to:
- citrus farms
- animal farms
- horse stables

Children can interact with animals and learn about rural life.

### Chocolate Factory Visit
An excursion to a local chocolate factory.
Children learn:
- how chocolate is made
- history of chocolate in Spain

Includes tasting session.

## Birthday Events
Asturio Club organizes custom birthday events for children:
- themed quests
- guided activities
- outdoor games

Programs are adapted to age and group size.

## Safety
All kids tours are conducted with safety as a top priority.
