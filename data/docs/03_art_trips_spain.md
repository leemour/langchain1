# Art Trips and Cultural Experiences — Asturio Club

Asturio Club organizes art-focused trips and cultural excursions across Spain.

## Art and Culture направления

### Museums and Art Centers
- Prado Museum (Madrid)
- Reina Sofía Museum (Madrid)
- Picasso Museum (Barcelona)
- IVAM (Valencia)

Guides provide context about:
- Spanish art history
- famous artists
- historical background of artworks

### Architecture Tours
Special focus on:
- Gothic architecture
- Modernism (especially in Barcelona)
- Contemporary Spanish architecture

### Art Trips for Adults
Designed for travelers interested in:
- art history
- painting
- sculpture
- architecture

These trips include in-depth explanations and slower pace.

## Educational Value
Art trips are suitable for:
- adults
- students
- teenagers interested in art and history

Some programs include interactive discussions and Q&A sessions.
