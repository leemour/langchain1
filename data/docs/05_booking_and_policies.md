## Booking Process
Tours and excursions can be booked:
- via website
- via direct message
- by prior agreement

Advance booking is recommended, especially during high season.

## Payment
Payment methods may include:
- bank transfer
- cash (by agreement)

Payment details are confirmed individually for each booking.

## Cancellation Policy
- Cancellation conditions depend on the type of tour.
- Some excursions may require prepayment.
- Late cancellations may incur a fee.

## Group Size Policy
- Minimum group size may apply for some excursions.
- Private tours are available.

## Weather Conditions
Some outdoor excursions depend on weather.
In case of bad weather, tours may be rescheduled or adjusted.
