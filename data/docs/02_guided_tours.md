# Guided Tours Across Spain — Asturio Club

Asturio Club offers guided tours not only in Valencia but also in other major cities and regions of Spain.

## Available Cities
- Barcelona
- Madrid
- Valencia
- Alicante
- Castellón

## Types of Tours

### City Sightseeing Tours
Classic city tours covering main landmarks, history, and culture.
Suitable for first-time visitors.

### Thematic Tours
Focused on specific topics:
- architecture
- history of Spain
- gastronomy
- local traditions

### Custom Tours
Tours tailored to client interests, schedule, and group composition.
Popular for families and small private groups.

## Tour Languages
Tours are available in:
- Russian
- English

## Tour Format
- Walking tours
- Car-based tours
- Combination of walking and transport

## Booking Notes
Tours can be booked in advance.
Last-minute bookings are possible depending on guide availability.
